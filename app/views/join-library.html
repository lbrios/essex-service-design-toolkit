    Joining the library service is free and easy. It gives you access to:

    • Books,e-books and e-magazines
    • Online courses and resources
    • Library computers and Wifi

    You don’t need any identification to join the library.

    Joining can be done online or in person in any of the Essex libraries.

    Join online

    Completing the registration form will give you an ‘Adult temp’ membership. You will receive a registration number by email and will be able to:

    • search the library catalogue immediately
    • reserve items that can be sent to any library in Essex
