    Joining the library service is free and easy. It gives you access to:

    • Books,e-books and e-magazines
    • Online courses and resources
    • Library computers and Wifi

    You don’t need any identification to join the library.

    Join online

    Completing the online registration form will give you an ‘Adult temp’ membership. You will receive a registration number by email and will be able to:

    • search the library catalogue immediately
    • reserve items that can be sent to any library in Essex

    Join online

    Join at the library

    Take your registration number into your local library if you want to:

    • collect your library card and any reserved items
    • change your membership type - if child, young adult, memory, access or friends and family membership is more appropriate
    • take advantage of the full range of library services, including e-books and e-magazines.

    Find your local library

    Pop along to your local library where you will be given membership and your library card.
    Please note: You can’t join the library over the phone.

    Lost library card or reactivate account

    You will need to come into a library to get a replacement card or to reactivate your account.

    You must bring identification with your name and address on it such as a driving licence, passport or recent utility bill.

    There is a charge of £1.05 for replacing lost or damaged library cards.

    Update your account details

    You can update your contact details or password by logging into your account on the Essex Libraries catalogue.
