Essex Schools Waiting List

You can check your child's current position on the waiting list of any Essex secondary school for Year 7 for September 2019.

You will need your child's student ID number. This can be found under "your ref" on the letter you received about your application. If you no longer have this letter, please call 0345 603 2200 to obtain your child's student ID.

Waiting lists are updated throughout the working day, as the waiting list changes.
