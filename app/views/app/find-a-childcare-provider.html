Additional Information
Our setting is called PIPS Nursery, ages range from three months to three years. The setting is based in my home so that the children benefit from a home from home environment. I am a Norland nurse and believe in traditional values. I employ up to nine members of staff who work part time (so there are always four members of staff plus myself each day).  I believe children of this age group need plenty of one to one attention throughout the day so we are able to give them this during their time here. The children enjoy plenty of fresh air and exercise here in Strethall which is on a farm four miles from Saffron Walden. Every child is an individual with their own special strengths and weaknesses.  Here at PIPS they enjoy developing their own strengths and are given every opportunity to flourish and to reach their potential.  As early years practitioners we can help build on the areas where they are less confident and secure.  We cannot make the world free of discrimination and a safe place to be - but we can do our very best to insure that our early years setting is a small model of what we would like the world to be, where children are safe, valued and included.
