    You can renew an item up to seven times. After this it must be returned to a library. You might be able to borrow it again if it has not been reserved by someone else.

    You can check the dates your items are due for return and pay any charges by logging into your account.

    You will need to pay a further hire charge if you renew CDs or DVDs.

    Remember: There are library charges for overdue books, CDs and DVDs so make sure that you renew your items if you want to keep them for longer.
