    You can apply for a September 2019 primary (Reception) school place between 12 November 2018 and 15 January 2019.

    Offers of places will be sent on 16 April 2019.

    Apply for a primary school place

    Before you apply you should:

    • check you live in the Essex administrative area
    • read the admissions booklet and leaflet (available from 12 November 2018)

    Apply for a primary school place

    Change your application

    If you make any changes to your application, you must re-submit the form by the closing date.

    After the closing date you will only be able to make changes to your address, not your preferences.
