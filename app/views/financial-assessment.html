Start your financial assessment

A financial assessment tells us how much you can afford to pay towards your care and support.

It can only be completed if you have already been assessed as needing care and support. If you haven't, you can request a care needs assessment

If you haven't had a care needs assessment, you can estimate your eligibility for financial support towards the cost of care with our simple, 5-minute financial checker.

Before you start

You will need to have some documents handy, such as bank statements and utility bills. See the full list of documents needed.

You can save your answers as you go and return to the form later.

It takes around 45 minutes to complete.
