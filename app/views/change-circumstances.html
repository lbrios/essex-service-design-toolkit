  • Paying for care

    • Update your details

    You need to tell us as soon as possible if there are any changes to your details, including:

    • name
    • address
    • bank account
    • financial situation, such as benefits entitlement

    Contact the Financial Assessment Team on 03330 135895 or email assessandpay.servicecentre@essex.gov.uk

    Direct payments

    You need to tell us if you have to go to hospital or temporary care if you receive direct payments. This is so we can make changes to your payments until you need care again.

    Contact the Direct Payments Team on 0345 603 7630 or email DirectPayments.FinancialPayments@essex.gov.uk

    Benefits

    If you receive benefits, you will need to tell your benefits provider about changes to your details. Visit GOV.UK for more information about benefits.
