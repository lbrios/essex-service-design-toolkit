    Changes to your details

    You need to tell your social worker if there are changes to your:

    • name
    • address
    • bank account

    This is so we can update our records.

    You need to tell your social worker if you:

    • need the payments to stop
    • go into hospital or residential care

    This helps us to make changes to your payments until you need care again.

    You also need to tell us if your financial circumstances change. This may make a difference to how much you have to pay towards your care and support.

    Suspending or ending direct payments

    You can choose to end direct payments at any time if:

    • you're no longer able to manage the direct payment
    • you no longer need the care and support which you're using direct payments for

    We will suspend or end your direct payments if:
