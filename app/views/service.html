Report a concern about a child

If you believe someone is in immediate danger, dial 999.

Contact Essex Safeguarding Children Board

www.escb.co.uk
Telephone: 0345 603 7627
Monday to Friday, 9:30am to 5:30pm

You may want to report a concern if you:

  • are worried about the safety or wellbeing of a child
  • suspect neglect or abuse
  • would like to report an incident

Visit the NSPCC to find out more about the signs of abuse and neglect.

Even if you are not sure whether we need to be aware, or whether it's serious or not, call us.

We are best placed to decide whether it warrants further investigation.
