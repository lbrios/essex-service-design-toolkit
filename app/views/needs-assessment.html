Care needs assessment

We might arrange for a social worker or occupational therapist to visit you to do a care needs assessment. They will speak to you in more detail about your care and support needs and advise you on the next steps.

Care and support plan

If you are assessed as needing social care and support, a social care worker will agree a care and support plan with you. The plan gives details of:

  • how your support will be provided
  • how much it costs
  • who will be paying for it

You will then be aksed to complete a financial assessment.

If you are not eligible for support, we will tell you about organisations that can help you.
