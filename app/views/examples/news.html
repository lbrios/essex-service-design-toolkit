Bake Off contestant at Cressing Chocolate Festival

We can now announce that Jon Jenkins, who appeared on last year's Great British Bake Off, will be at Cressing Chocolate Festival on 19 April.

Jon, who became well-known for his love of bright shirts, made it to week seven on Bake Off, creating a number of fantastic bakes along the way.

If you want some top tips, Jon will be making an Easter-themed chocolate ball reveal and a chocolate collar around a cake. He will also be hosting a question and answer session, so anyone attending the workshop will be able to find out more about his techniques and experience on the show.
